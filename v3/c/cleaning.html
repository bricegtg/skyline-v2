Scope

If it's vertical, fragile, or far above the street — we clean it.

Eight building categories, one platform. Each project starts with a free aerial survey to confirm fit, hazards, and CAAP airspace clearance.

01High-rise residentialCondominium towers and luxury developments. Exterior glass, balcony walls, signage, and fins from 20 to 160 metres.
02Commercial office towersGrade-A BGC and Makati offices. Curtain-wall facades on quarterly cycles, documented before/after for property managers.
03Solar arrays & rooftopsIndustrial and utility-scale solar. Drone wash restores 5–15% lost generation — usually within a single morning.
04Stadium & arena roofsMembrane and metal roofs at arena scale. We clean the unreachable spans between truss bays without roof anchors.
05Bridges & infrastructureCable-stayed and steel-arch bridges. We wash structural members and inspect coatings for pre-painting prep and audits.
06Industrial & warehouseLogistics hubs, food-grade facilities, factory roofs. We strip dust and bird soil that compromise fire sensors and skylights.
07Heritage & stone claddingLimestone, granite, terracotta. Pressure-controlled cleaning that preserves jointing, documented per panel.
08Petrochemical & plantTank exteriors, flare stacks, cooling-tower fans. Contactless cleaning avoiding hot-work permits and confined-space entry.
The four-part system

It's not a hose with rotors. It's a precision system.

Reverse-osmosis purified water. 30–60 PSI through a soft-jet nozzle. RTK station-hold to two centimetres, even in gusts. Every flight logged automatically and delivered as a PDF.
