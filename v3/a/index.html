The Skyline method

Nobody should die cleaning a window.

We rebuilt the whole job around a machine that holds station to two centimetres, sprays purified water at calibrated pressure, and lands when the crew says so. The operator stays on the ground. The drone does the height.

How it works

From brief to final flight report in five steps.

01
Site brief

You send a building photo, the floor count, and what's not working. We respond within 24 hours with a proposal range.

02
Aerial survey

Free 30-minute drone walk-around. We capture facade condition, flag hazards and CAAP airspace constraints before quoting.

03
Fixed-scope quote

Per-floor or per-panel pricing. No hidden lift mobilisation, no scaffold day-rate. What you sign is what we bill.

04
Flight day

Crew of three on site: lead pilot, support pilot, ground safety. 60-minute flight blocks until the surface is signed off.

05
Flight report

A delivery PDF: before/after photography, area covered, water and chemical usage, flight logs, recommended next interval.
