Common questions

Before you send the brief.

Are you CAAP-certified?

Yes — Skyline operates under a full CAAP Drone Operator Certificate and carries third-party aviation liability for every flight. We attach the certificate copy on request before signing.

What does the free aerial survey cover?

A 30-minute on-site flight: surface mapping, hazard photo log, airspace clearance check, and a 1-page recommendation. Surveys are free for cleaning projects we expect to take on; large-portfolio surveys are quoted separately.

Can I buy the IND-160-F outright?

Yes. The IND-160-F is sold as a complete deployment package — airframe, suppression payload system, ground station, pilot certification, and a maintenance contract. Select "IND-160-F — procurement" above and we'll send pricing and lead times.

Do you fly outside Metro Manila?

Yes. We've operated in Cebu, Davao, Bataan, La Union, and Pampanga. Out-of-region cleaning projects include travel and mob/demob in the fixed quote. No hidden line items.

What does an aerial photography shoot include?

4K / 60fps capture on a stabilised gimbal, a CAAP-cleared flight plan, a Remote Pilot in Command on every flight, same-day proxies, and a colour-graded final reel within the agreed window. Select “Aerial photography / videography” above and send a shot list to scope a quote.

Can the IND-160-F integrate with our BFP unit?

Always. We deliver an operations briefing pack and run a joint drill before first deployment. For BFP partnerships, our pilots integrate into the incident-command structure under the unit's operations chief.

What do you need from us to quote?

For cleaning: a facade photo, the address, floor count, and surface type. For the IND-160-F: deployment site, expected scenarios, and quantity. That's enough to produce pricing within a week.
