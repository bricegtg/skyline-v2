The fire is won or lost in the first six minutes.

A ladder truck takes time to arrive, set up, and tops out around the seventh floor. Everything above it is left waiting while the fire grows.

Those first minutes decide whether a contained incident becomes a catastrophe — and they are exactly the minutes traditional appliances cannot serve at height.

02

Airborne in six. Reaching one hundred and sixty metres.

The IND-160-F puts water or foam on a high-rise, a refinery tank, or a wildland front while the first crew is still mobilising.

Thermal sensing finds the seat of the fire before a single litre is committed, so every drop lands where it changes the outcome.
