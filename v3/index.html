One brand. Three ways to fly it.

The same content, tokens, and signature rotor mark — rendered in three distinct directions. Preview each, then choose the one that fits how Skyline should feel to a procurement officer, a property owner, and a fire chief.

Pure Editorial
A

Pure Editorial

Maximum restraint. One enormous idea per section, viewport-filling display type, generous whitespace, and a single quiet scroll reveal. Gold and ember never share a section.

Best for

A confident, gallery-like brand that reads as premium and unhurried — closest to high-end architecture and design studios.

Cinematic Industrial
B

Cinematic Industrial

Full-bleed dark cinema. Hero clip-path reveal, pinned crossfade chapters, horizontal-scroll showcases, and full-screen gold and ember breaks. The most dramatic motion.

Best for

Maximum impact and a story-driven scroll — ideal when the IND-160-F and the fleet need to feel powerful and inevitable.

View this variant
Hybrid · Recommended
C

Hybrid

The recommended balance. Home, cleaning, and contact stay calm and editorial; the IND-160-F page switches to cinematic chapters where drama earns its place.

Best for

A brand that stays composed for everyday buyers but turns up the intensity for the flagship product — the safest, most versatile choice.
