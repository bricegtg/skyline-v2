Frequently asked

Answers we get most.

How fast can you mobilise for a cleaning brief?

Survey within 72 hours of brief. Full operation typically inside two weeks for portfolio retainers, longer for first-time properties due to insurance and building management protocols. Permits, NOTAMs, and BMS coordination are handled by us.

Is the IND-160-F sold turnkey, or is training separate?

Turnkey. Every unit ships with a two-week pilot certification programme, annual recertification, and depot-grade spare-parts logistics. Your team flies the platform; we keep it airworthy.

Do you respond to live fires?

No. We sell the platform — we don't run the response. Your team, BFP, or industrial first-responders are the operators. Our role is engineering, training, and platform support.

What's a typical cleaning engagement cost?

Single-cycle projects start at PHP 180k for a costed photogrammetric survey plus one full surface wash. Property retainers start at PHP 120k per quarter. Multi-asset programmes are priced against scope.

How is the IND-160-F priced?

Configuration-dependent. Indicative bands ship with the spec sheet. A procurement call delivers a costed proposal inside five working days. Multi-unit and municipal fleet discounts apply.

Where do you operate?

Headquartered in Manila. Active engagements across the Philippines, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and Qatar. Cleaning brief response is fastest in the NCR and central Luzon. Procurement support is global.
