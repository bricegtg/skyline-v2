01 Why this platform exists

The first six minutes of a fire are fought by people on the ground. They shouldn't be.

A traditional aerial ladder reaches 30 metres on a good day. The hot zone of a Manila high-rise fire is at 80 metres, ten storeys above where the longest articulated truck can spray.

The IND-160-F was built for those storeys. It reaches the fire before the ladder. It clears air for the people in the building. And it does it without putting a single firefighter in the smoke.

We don't run the response. Your team does. We build the platform that makes the response possible.

03 · Capabilities

Six fire profiles. One airframe.

Configurable for the threats your jurisdiction actually faces — high-rise, industrial, wildland, settlement, infrastructure, transport.

CAPABILITY · 01

High-rise & tower

Reaches storeys 12–50 directly. Pre-clears air for evacuation. Optional foam, water mist, or PPC suppressant.

CAPABILITY · 02

Industrial & refinery

Foam delivery for hydrocarbon fires. Thermal mapping for hot-spot containment. Hazard-distance compliant.

CAPABILITY · 03

Wildland & settlement

Drop-and-return tank cycling for fire-break maintenance. Mapped suppressant delivery against modelled spread.

CAPABILITY · 04

Infrastructure & transport

Bridge, tunnel-mouth, tanker, port. Pre-deploy from a fixed mooring with 6-minute scramble.

CAPABILITY · 05

Recon & thermal

640px thermal + RGB stack. Live downlink to incident command. Auto-target marking for ground response.

CAPABILITY · 06

Training & certification

Two-week pilot certification programme. Annual recertification. Spare-parts and depot service included.
