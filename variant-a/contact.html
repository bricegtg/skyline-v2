Common questions

Before you send the brief.

Are you CAAP-certified?

Yes — Skyline operates under a full CAAP Drone Operator Certificate and carries third-party aviation liability for every flight. We attach the certificate copy on request before signing.

What does the free aerial survey cover?

A 30-minute on-site flight: surface mapping, hazard photo log, airspace clearance check, and a 1-page recommendation. Surveys are free for projects we expect to take on; large-portfolio surveys are quoted separately.

How long until you can deploy?

Cleaning: typically a 2–4 week lead from signed quote to first flight. Firefighting: pre-positioned partners get on-call response within a defined SLA. New industrial firefighting partnerships take 6–10 weeks of joint drilling first.

Do you fly outside Metro Manila?

Yes. We've operated in Cebu, Davao, Bataan, La Union, and Pampanga. Out-of-region projects include travel and mob/demob in the fixed quote. No hidden line items.

Can you work alongside our HSE team / BFP unit?

Always. We deliver an HSE briefing pack and run a joint drill before the first flight. For BFP partnerships, our pilots integrate into the incident-command structure under the unit's operations chief.

What do you need from us to quote?

A photo of the facade or site, the address, the floor count or building dimensions, and the surface type. That's enough to produce a fixed-price quote within a week.
