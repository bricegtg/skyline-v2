Who We Are

INTRODUCING SKYLINE DRONES

Skyline Drones is a Manila-based aerial services company deploying autonomous drone fleets across two divisions — exterior building cleaning and aerial firefighting support.

Our commercial-grade fleet services high-rises, hotels, government, airports, industrial facilities and public infrastructure — delivering faster, safer and more cost-effective alternatives to rope-access and scaffolding methods.

Whether it's a 5-storey office or a 55-storey tower, Skyline provides full-facade coverage at a fraction of the time and cost — with zero human risk.

The Problem

WHY BUILDING OWNERS ARE LOOKING
FOR A BETTER WAY

High Liability

Legal & Insurance Exposure

Rope-access and scaffolding crews work at extreme heights daily — exposing owners and facility managers to significant liability, premiums, and regulatory risk.

Excessive Cost

Inflated Maintenance Budgets

Traditional cleaning needs scaffolding, cranes, lifts, or rope teams — all demanding setup, specialised labour, and premium insurance. The result: bloated budgets.

Operational Disruption

Weeks of Scaffolding

Scaffolding blocks entrances, obstructs signage, disrupts tenants, and takes weeks to erect and dismantle — prolonged friction for buildings and tenants alike.
