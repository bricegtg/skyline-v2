Scope

If it's vertical, fragile, or far above the street — we clean it.

Eight building categories, one platform. Each project starts with a free aerial survey to confirm fit, hazards, and CAAP airspace clearance.

01

High-rise residential

Condominium towers, mixed-use podiums, and luxury developments. We clean exterior glass, balcony walls, signage, and architectural fins from 20 to 160 metres.

02

Commercial office towers

Grade-A BGC and Makati offices. We clean curtain-wall facades on quarterly cycles, with documented before/after PDFs for property managers and tenants.

03

Solar arrays & rooftops

Industrial and utility-scale solar. Drone wash restores 5–15% lost generation from dust and bird soil — usually within a single morning, no panel-by-panel teardown.

04

Stadium & arena roofs

Membrane and metal roofs at PhilSports, Mall of Asia Arena scale. We clean the unreachable spans between truss bays without scaffolding or roof-anchor inspection.

Cable-stayed and steel-arch bridges. We wash structural members and inspect coatings — useful for pre-painting prep and government infrastructure audits.

06

Industrial & warehouse

Logistics hubs, food-grade facilities, factory roofs. We strip dust accumulation and bird soil that would otherwise compromise fire-detection sensors and skylight transmission.

07

Heritage & stone cladding

Limestone, granite, terracotta. Pressure-controlled cleaning that preserves jointing. Documented per-panel for restoration architects and conservation officers.

08

Petrochemical & plant

Tank exteriors, flare stacks, cooling-tower fans. Contactless cleaning avoiding hot-work permits and confined-space entry. Reviewed against client HSE protocol.

Method

It's not a hose with rotors. It's a precision system.

Skyline drones spray a purified-water + low-residue detergent mix through a 360°-articulating nozzle, at 30–60 PSI calibrated to the surface. The drone holds station against wind via RTK GPS, and a tethered fluid line keeps payload weight low so we can fly longer cycles.

  • Purified water

    Reverse-osmosis on-site. Leaves zero mineral spotting — no rinse pass needed.

  • Calibrated pressure

    30–60 PSI delivered through a soft-jet nozzle. Safe for glass, sealants, stone, and solar.

  • RTK station-hold

    2 cm horizontal accuracy. The drone tracks a vertical line without drift, even in 30 km/h gusts.

  • Tethered fluid line

    Continuous supply from a ground-based mixing rig. No mid-flight refill. Longer effective cycle.

  • Full flight log

    Telemetry, photo, and area-coverage data captured automatically. Delivered as PDF post-job.
