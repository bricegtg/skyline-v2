The Rig

The IND-160-F.

One platform.
Thermal-guided.

Twin-rotor industrial UAV with 500-litre suppression payload, thermal targeting lance, and continuous tank-swap support. Standoff distance for crew. Direct line to the seat of fire.

  • Thermal-guided suppression lance — water, foam, or dry-chemical loadout.
  • Continuous tank exchange via ground-tether or relay rig — no flight to refill.
  • BFP-allied dispatch protocol. Co-deployed with municipal engines and ladder trucks.
  • Real-time thermal map shared to incident command from first lift-off.
  • CAAP-permitted and insured to USD 5M per incident.
Dispatch Protocol Six minutes

From your call to
first-water.

T+0:00

Dispatch

Call received. Incident triaged. Crew paged on hot-standby pattern.

T+1:30

Mobilise

Rig and tank-swap relay stage on-route. NOTAM filed in flight.

T+3:00

Launch

Lift-off from staging vehicle. Thermal map streamed to incident command.

T+5:30

Suppress

First-water on the seat. Tank-swap relay engaged for sustained drops.

T+90:00

Handover

Incident closed to ground crew. Thermal log + insurance pack filed.
