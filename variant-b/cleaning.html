The Rig

One platform.
Calibrated per surface.

The IND-160-C.

Twin-rotor industrial UAV with 500-litre solvent payload, programmable nozzle array, and on-board pressure modulation. Every surface gets its own recipe.

  • Non-streak, certified-safe solvents — calibrated to glass, panel, membrane or stone.
  • Drainage capture at grade — zero contamination of public realm, pedestrians or plant.
  • Photogrammetric pre- and post-cycle capture, delivered with the insurance pack.
  • Tethered or autonomous, depending on payload and wind window.
  • CAAP-permitted, NOTAM-issued, briefed to property and tenants the day before.
